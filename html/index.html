Vellore Institute of Technology is a private research deemed university located in Katpadi in Vellore, India.
Founded in 1984 as Vellore Engineering College by G. Viswanathan, the institution offers 66 Undergraduate,
58 Postgraduate, 15 Integrated, 2 Research and 2 M.Tech Industrial Programmes.
